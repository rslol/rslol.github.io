New Volcanoes

A client came to Timbr Technologies and requested a new site for their music. Seeing this as an opportunity to do some pair programming, a fellow programmer and I created this visually appealing, user friendly website that allows the New Volcanoes to showcase their music, events, and pictures to their fans. Made the site with HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery for the Front-End and NodeJS/ExpressJS/MongoDB for the Back-End. Authentication was handled by PassportJS.

Andrea's Corner

My best friend is a animator and decided to create a webcomic based off of her day to day experiences. While she uses Facebook and Instagram to reach her audience, I realized she should have own website where she can upload her comics and also give visitors a little insight to who she is as a person. I built the Front-End with HTML/CSS/Javascript and the backend I used NodeJS/ExpressJS/MongoDB. Authentication was also built by PassportJS. I definitely am not recognizing a pattern at all.
