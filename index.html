New Volcanoes

A client came to Timbr Technologies and requested a new site for their music. Seeing this as an opportunity to do some pair programming, a fellow programmer and I created this visually appealing, user friendly website that allows the New Volcanoes to showcase their music, events, and pictures to their fans. Made the site with HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery for the Front-End and NodeJS/ExpressJS/MongoDB for the Back-End. Deployed with Heroku
